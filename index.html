Raghav Nagarajan

Any discussion on math,music(#desi) and money excites Raghav. He is a true believer in value investing, does his own research and takes his time to invest his money into anything. Raghav has done his bachelors and masters in Mathematics from IIT Kharagpur. He has worked with several Indian and Global financial services firms like Barclays Capital, Edelweiss, Crisil in a diversified set of roles covering all facets of operations, technology and front office.He has cleared CFA(certified financial analyst) level 1 and 2, all 5 examinations of CFP(certified financial planner) certification, CWM(certified wealth manager) level 1 and 2. “Keep it simple” - he says.

Varun Mundra

If you want to befriend Varun, talk about poker ( strictly Texas holdem ). A true believer in high risk, high return strategies but cautions you to always maintain a stop loss.Varun has done his bachelors in computer science from IIT Kharagpur.
